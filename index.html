Step 2. Add Your Signature

Written Instructions

Step 1: Fill Out Required Fields

  • Open the email signature generator tool.
  • Fill out all required fields marked in red.

Step 2: Enter Your Name

  • Include your salutation (e.g., Mr., Ms., Dr.).
  • If you are an alumnus, add your graduation year at the end of your name.
    Example: Mr. Harry the Hornet '54

Step 3: Enter Your Title(s)

  • For your primary title, use the official title listed on the website directory.
  • Make sure it follows your department’s standard format.
  • Optionally, enter a secondary title (e.g., coach, club sponsor, etc.).

Step 4: Contact Info

  • If applicable, add your phone extension.
  • Enter your email username (do not include @bishopmoore.org).
    Example: if your email is jdoe@bishopmoore.org, just type jdoe.

Step 5: Preview and Download

  • Review the preview to ensure all information is accurate and fits properly.
  • Click “Download Image” to save your signature to your desktop.

Step 6: Add Signature to Gmail

  • Open Gmail settings by clicking the Gear Icon at the top right → “See all settings.”
  • Scroll to the Signature section → click “Create new.”
  • Click the image icon → choose Google Drive → click “Upload” and upload your signature image.

Step 7: Fix Access Permissions

  • Open Google Drive and locate your uploaded image.
  • Right-click the image → click “Share.”
  • Under “General Access,” select “Anyone with the link.”

Step 8: Insert and Format Signature

  • Return to Gmail settings → click the image icon again.
  • Select the image from My Drive and insert it.
  • Set the image size to Large.
  • Click after the image and press Enter a few times to preserve spacing.

Step 9: Save and Finalize

  • Select the new signature as your default (if you have other signatures).
  • Click “Save Changes.”
  • Compose a new email to confirm your signature appears correctly.
